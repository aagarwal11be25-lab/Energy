Stubble Burning in India

A Critical Environmental Challenge

What is Stubble Burning?

Stubble burning is the practice of intentionally setting fire to crop residue (mainly rice and wheat straw) left in agricultural fields after harvesting. This practice is widespread in northern India, particularly in Punjab, Haryana, and Uttar Pradesh.
